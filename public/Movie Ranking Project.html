Leeroy's Movies - The Best of the Best!

My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers: Endgame

This movie had all the elements of winning an Oscar Award [even though it did not]. As a Marvel Studios fan, I enjoyed this movie because of the storyline and the action scenes and the emotional rollercoaster that it made me go through. It will always be my best movie.

Terminator 2: Judgement Day

A cyborg, a kid, and a badass Linda Hamilton take on a liquid metal nightmare! Explosive action, iconic one-liners, and the ultimate motorcycle chase. Still one of the coolest sequels ever—Hasta la vista, boredom!

Gran Turismo

From gamer to racer, this adrenaline-fueled ride proves virtual skills can pay off! High-speed thrills, heart-pounding races, and a story that feels too wild to be true (but it is!). Buckle up—this one is a winner!
